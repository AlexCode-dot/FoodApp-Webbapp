Welcome to Your Food Planner!

Are you ready to take control of your meals and shopping list? Our app makes meal planning easier, more organized, and fun! Here's how:

  • Organize Your Meals:

    Choose from various meal types (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, or Snacks) and add your favorite recipes.

  • Create a Shopping List:

    Add ingredients to your shopping list with a single click, and check off items as you go. Never forget an item again!

  • Stay in Control:

    Filter your food items by type, search for specific meals, and keep your shopping list updated in real-time.

  • Save Your Favorites:

    Your food and shopping lists are saved, so you can return anytime to view or edit them.

This is more than just a food app — it’s a simple way to stay organized, reduce food waste, and enjoy healthy, well-planned meals. Start planning today and make your kitchen time easier!
